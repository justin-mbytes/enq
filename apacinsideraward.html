Kinetic Education wins 2018 APAC Insider Australian Enterprise Award - Best Online Education & Tutoring Platform

Kinetic Education is pleased to announce we have won the 2018 APAC Insider Australian Enterprise Award - Best in Maths and English Tutor Services 2018.

Kinetic Education are dedicated to providing the highest quality of literacy and numeracy products, making them accessible to all school age children, so that they can have better career choices and an improved quality of life.

Hosted by APAC Insider, the Australian Enterprise Awards (AEA) acknowledges businesses and individuals who play a pivotal role in the resurgence of Australia's economy. APAC Insider also produces a quarterly supplement covering business topics and profiling businesses across the Asia Pacific region.

The listing for Kinetic Education can be found at
