Maths Wiz & English Wiz

Maths Wiz and English Wiz are central to the Kinetic Education tutoring solution. The programs include over 35,000 interactive questions, skill building games and tests (including NAPLAN preparation) supporting school. Using Maths Wiz and English Wiz, students are able to learn much faster (in fact teachers and principals have reported, concepts that may take up to 2 weeks to learn in the classroom can be learnt in just 20 minutes on the program) due to its interactive nature.

Maths Doctor is a very clever assessment which makes a personal Weekly Lesson Plan, based on your child’s strengths and weaknesses. This comes up automatically in Maths Wiz, you don’t have to do a thing. Furthermore, your Weekly Lesson Plans are updated every week, based on results. So your children can truly reach their true potential!

Our tutors undertake extensive training on the programs, so that we can confidently provide support to our parents and students. We use the feedback from parents and our interaction with students to constantly improve the programs and develop ways to improve the way we can support our families. When Maths Wiz and English Wiz is combined with support from our tutor team, it truly is a most effective and affordable tutoring solution.

Science Wiz

Biology, Chemistry and Physics for Years 11 and 12

With Science, you need to learn a lot of new vocabulary, facts, theories, calculations and (above all) how to think scientifically. In all the sciences, there is a lot to remember, but just memorising is not enough. Students can feel quite lost without extra help.
