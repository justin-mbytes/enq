ABOUT

The Kinetic Education Story

The inspiration for Kinetic Education dates back to 1985 to a husband and wife team whose own child was having difficulties learning Maths. Kinetic Education is the marketing arm of M-Bytes Pty Ltd, which is a family business founded by Mary and Bharat Sanghvi. Mary, an experienced Maths teacher and editor, teamed up with her husband Bharat, a Maths graduate and computer programmer, to write some simple programs to help their own child with his mathematics. They were astounded by how quickly their son picked up the concepts from using the computer.

The company now employs a team of professional tutors and educators who are dedicated to helping students on four continents to achieve their full potential.

The lesson design and teaching pedagogy have been developed in consultation with teachers, principals, university lecturers, educational experts and psychologists.

Learning with Maths & English Wiz is structured and multi-sensory. The Maths Wiz program teaches your child how to "think" Maths so that they gain confidence with their school work. Our friendly tutors, who are only a phone call away, closely monitor your child's progress and provide advice and support so that you can be confident that your child is on the right track.

Our Mission

Dedicated to providing the highest quality of literacy and numeracy products, making them accessible to all school age children, so that they can have better career choices and an improved quality of life.
